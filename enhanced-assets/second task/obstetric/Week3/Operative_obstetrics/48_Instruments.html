Uterine Sound

PropertyDetail
Material/designMalleable
Tip diameter3 to 4 mm
Tip shapeBlunt
ScaleGraduated
ShapeCurved

Indications:

  • Measuring cervical and uterine length
  • Diagnosis of cervical stenosis
  • Determining direction and position of uterus
COMPLICATIONS
Shock · Perforation

Uterine Curette

Types: Blunt and sharp.

Indications:

  • Evacuation of the uterus in cases of incomplete or missed abortion
  • Abnormal uterine bleeding
COMPLICATIONS
As uterine sound plus intestinal injury

Special Forceps

Green Armytage Forceps

  • Hold the edges of uterine incision in cesarean section
INSTRUMENT · img-05
Green Armytage forceps — holds edges of uterine incision in CS.
Source: Book Obstetrics 2025-2026, p. 261

Ring Forceps

  • Has lock
  • Used to grasp cervix in pregnant uterus during cerclage and curettage
  • Remove products of conception
  • Remove polyps
INSTRUMENT · img-06a
Ring forceps — has lock, used to grasp cervix in pregnant uterus during cerclage and curettage.
Source: Book Obstetrics 2025-2026, p. 262

Ovum Forceps

  • Like ring forceps but has no lock
  • Remove products of conception
INSTRUMENT · img-06a
Ovum forceps — used to remove products of conception (like ring forceps but no lock).
