Uterine Sound

PropertyDetail
Material/designMalleable
Tip diameter3 to 4 mm
Tip shapeBlunt
ScaleGraduated
ShapeCurved

Indications:

  • Measuring cervical and uterine length
  • Diagnosis of cervical stenosis
  • Determining direction and position of uterus
COMPLICATIONS
Shock · Perforation

Cervical Dilators

Types:

  1. Hegar — Graduated dilators
  2. Fenton — Alternative design

Indications:

  • Dilatation of the cervix to facilitate intrauterine surgery
COMPLICATIONS
Neurogenic shock · Infection · Perforation · Cervical lacerations
INSTRUMENT · img-03
Cervical dilators — Hegar and Fenton types.
Source: Book Obstetrics 2025-2026, p. 260
4

Uterine Curette

Types: Blunt and sharp.

Indications:

  • Evacuation of the uterus in cases of incomplete or missed abortion
  • Abnormal uterine bleeding
COMPLICATIONS
As uterine sound plus intestinal injury
INSTRUMENT · img-04
Uterine curette — blunt and sharp types for evacuation of the uterus.
Source: Book Obstetrics 2025-2026, p. 261

Ring Forceps

  • Has lock
  • Used to grasp cervix in pregnant uterus during cerclage and curettage
  • Remove products of conception
  • Remove polyps
